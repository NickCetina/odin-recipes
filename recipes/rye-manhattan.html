Rye Manhattan

DESCRIPTION

Classic and incredibly classy coctail made with rye whiskey.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup rye whiskey
  • 2 tablespoons sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 maraschino cherry

STEPS

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes.
  2. Pour in the whiskey, vermouth and bitters.
  3. Shake until frosty, about 15 seconds then strain into a Manhattan glass.
  4. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.
